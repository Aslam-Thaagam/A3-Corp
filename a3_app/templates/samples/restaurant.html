Our Story

A Legacy of Authentic Flavours

Sara was born in 2008 from a single dream — to bring the soul of Tamil Nadu's home kitchens to the dining table. Named after the Tamil word for "essence", every dish at Sara carries a story.

From the iconic Chettinad kitchens of Karaikudi to the coconut-laced flavours of Kerala, we honour every regional tradition with the finest local ingredients.

Fresh Ground Spices Daily
We grind our masalas every morning using traditional stone grinders — no packets, no shortcuts, only freshness.
Banana Leaf Dining Experience
Enjoy the authentic South Indian tradition of banana-leaf meals — a ritual of community, gratitude, and flavour.
Heritage Recipes, Modern Heart
Recipes handed down through three generations, now crafted in a warm, welcoming modern dining space.
