## Sales Pitch Tips

- Most businesses here are **single-owner operations** (85-95%) — decision maker is the owner themselves.
- Offer a **basic website package** (landing page + contact + Google Maps) as an entry point.
- Upsell to **mobile app**, **social media branding**, and **SEO** once trust is built.
- **Email addresses** are rarely public — call first, then ask for email and owner name directly.
- Best time to call: **10 AM – 12 PM** or **4 PM – 6 PM** (avoid lunch hours).
